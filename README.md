# 🌍 CarbonTwin AI

**CarbonTwin AI** is an AI-powered climate intelligence platform that helps users understand, track, predict, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, sustainability missions, and future impact simulations.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Many people want to live sustainably but do not fully understand how their daily activities impact the environment.

CarbonTwin AI addresses this challenge by creating an intelligent digital twin that tracks environmental impact, predicts future outcomes, and recommends practical actions to reduce emissions.

---

## 💡 Solution

CarbonTwin AI creates a personalized **AI Carbon Twin** that analyzes lifestyle patterns, visualizes environmental impact, predicts future climate outcomes, and motivates users through gamification and community engagement.

---
